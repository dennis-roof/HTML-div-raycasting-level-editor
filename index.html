HTML raycaster editor

    Features:
  • Real-time level editing (click inside the map)
  • Dynamic moving lights
  • Different height blocks
  • Rendering with 100% divs and CSS, no canvas
    Controls:
  • Move forward: W, arrow key up, click in middle or touch in middle
  • Move left: A, arrow key left, click in left area or touch in left area
  • Move right: D, arrow key right, click in right area or touch in right area
  • Jump: move over gap, jumps automatically
